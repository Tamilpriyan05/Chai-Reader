# Speak with Authors - Image Files

Place your author and book cover images here. The following files are expected:

## Author Portrait Images (Left side of card)
- `shakespeare.png`   → William Shakespeare portrait
- `marktwain.png`     → Mark Twain portrait  
- `suntzu.png`        → Sun Tzu portrait

## Book Cover Images (Right side of card)
- `tomsawyer.png`     → The Adventures of Tom Sawyer cover
- `artofwar.png`      → The Art of War cover
- `thirukkural.png`   → Thirukkural cover

## Recommended image sizes
- Author portraits: 300×400px minimum (portrait orientation)
- Book covers: 300×450px minimum (portrait orientation)
